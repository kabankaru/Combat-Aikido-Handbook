Combat Techniques

COMBAT NO. 1
Step 1: Step forward outside.
Step 2: Aikido hold then take down.
Step 3: Armlock then elbow crack.
COMBAT NO. 2
Step 1: Block overhead outside then elbow destruction.
Step 2: Aikido hold then take down.
Step 3: Disarm then strike and crack the attacker's hand in the front. With the position knee on the face and hips.
COMBAT NO. 3
Step 1: Block biceps inside.
Step 2: Elbow strike.
Step 3: Swift then step over the attacker and elbow chop.

COMBAT NO. 4

Step 1: Dodge then double block outside.
Step 2: Aikido hold and takedown.
Step 3: Elbow guide to the back. Elbow push.

COMBAT NO. 5

Step 1: Step forward and downward block outside.
Step 2: Aikido hold and takedown.
Step 3: Reverse Aikido hold and stab.

COMBAT NO. 6

Step 1: Side step outside and elbow cracking.
Step 2: DOS Aikido hold and takedown.
Step 3: Chicken wings.

COMBAT NO. 7

Step 1: Backstep block while pulling the hands then elbow strike.
Step 2: DOS Aikido hold and then take down.
Step 3: Knee on the face and hips then hand crack on the front.

COMBAT NO. 8

Step 1: Step forward then double block outside.
Step 2: Knee strike.
Step 3: DOS Aikido hold takedown and then chicken wings.

COMBAT NO. 9

Step 1: Step forward and overhead block outside.
Step 2: Automatic DOS Aikido hold and takedown.
Step 3: Step over the attacker then knee face strike and reverse chicken wings.
